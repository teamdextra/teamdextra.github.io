Dextra

Smart Hand Rehabilitation System

About the Product

Dextra is a low-cost hand rehabilitation system designed to assist patients in finger and wrist recovery. The device uses controlled movements and a mobile application to support therapy sessions at home.

Technical Limitations

  • Limited to predefined motion ranges
  • Bluetooth-dependent mobile control
  • Requires supervision during use
  • Not a replacement for professional therapy
